MiniGel Electrophoresis System

Useful for a variety of nucleic acid applications, the MiniGel Electrophoresis System is supplied complete withall components needed for agarose electrophoresis ofnucleic acids: two gel casting sets, gel tank and connect-ing power supply. The system is extremely versatile, withthree different sizes of gel trays, multiple combs and fouroutput voltage settings.

The gel tank is molded for leakproof performance and along service life. The power supply connects directly intothe gel tank, ensuring a proper connection is made everytime. The power supply connects to the gel box thoughthe lid - the lid cannot be removed without first discon-necting the power.

Casting three different sizes of gels is simple withthe supplied casting sets. No tape or even endgates are required. Multichannel compatiblecombs are also supplied. Additional gel trays,combs and casting sets are available.
