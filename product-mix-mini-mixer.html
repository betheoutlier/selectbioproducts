MiniMixer Blot Mixer
With a 5º angle and fixed 18/20 rpm fixed speeds, the MiniMixer Blot Mixer is optimally designed for processing blots and staining gels. The MiniMixer is supplied with a non-slip rubber mat, one large and one small blot box.

The MiniMixer is cold room and incubator compatible.
