MiniTwist Nutating Mixer
For samples requiring more thorough mixing than that provided by a platform rocker, the MiniTwist combines the actions of a rocker and an orbital shaker to produce a three dimensional motion.

The MiniTwist is ideal for mixing and resuspending samples in blood collection tubes as well as centrifuge tubes. The platform of the MiniTwist is covered with a dimpled mat that will hold up to 21 standard or 30 pediatric blood collection tubes, fifteen 15 ml or nine 50 ml centrifuge tubes or 60 microtubes.

Both the Rock-it Platform Rocker and MiniTwist Nutating Mixer can be operated in temperature controlled environments from 4º to 65ºC.
