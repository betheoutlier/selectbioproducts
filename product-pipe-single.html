Excel Electronic Pipettes
The multifunctional Excel Electronic Pipettes can be used for standard and reverse pipetting as well as multiple and sequential dispensing, mixing of samples and serial dilutions. Functions are easily programmed with one hand using the well positioned display and keypad. Excellent accuracy and reproducibility are guaranteed by the fully motorized piston drive and autocalibration function.

Excel Electronic Pipettes are comfortable for both right and left handed users. The units are lightweight and well balanced. Just a small movement of the index finger is required to activate the plunger button, reducing the risk of Repetitive Stress Injuries (RSI). Tip ejection requires only slight pressure on the ejector from the thumb.

Up to nine sets of parameters may be stored in the system memory. Aspirating and dispensing speed, audible signals and an ejection alarm are easily set. A graphical interface prompts the users for action. Self diagnostic software constantly monitors accuracy of pipetting volume, battery charge status and the autocalibration function.

A removable, rechargeable Lithium-ion battery powers the pipettes. The unit is supplied with a charger cord for charging the battery in the pipette. An optional charger set charges the battery outside of the pipette.		 Specifications
