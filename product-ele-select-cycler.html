SelectCycler™ II Thermal Cycler
The new Select Cycler II replaces our successful and reliable Select Cycler gradient thermal cycler and brings new speed and advanced features to our Select BioProducts Thermal Cycler line providing premium performance at an affordable price.

The Select Cycler II employs a new protocol optimization process improving on older gradient features. The Select Cycler II utilizes FlexTemp technology that effectively separates the 96 well plate into six (4 x 4) temperature regions that allow you to choose the temperatures. These regions are easily identified by the blue and black squares visible on the microplate block. Instead of having the thermal cycler choose temperatures for you, you can choose any temperatures you want within a 24°C range. The Select Cycler II has improved ramp rates that have reduced run times allowing for more productivity.

New is the PC Viewer Option. By downloading software from our website and attaching a USB cable to your Windows PC you can view the temperature profiles real time as they are occurring to monitor your system.

The Select Cycler II utilizes an intuitive user-friendly interface. The Select Cycler II comes pre-loaded with standard pre-programmed methods including those for Optimization, Touchdown, and Time Increments. To save time you can modify methods “on the fly”.

The Select Cycler II’s heated lid has been optimized to prevent condensation. For optimum performance, the lid can also be programmed to hold different temperatures between 60° to 65°C and 100° to 115°C.		 Specifications
