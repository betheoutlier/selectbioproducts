 Micro 18 R
High speed and durability define the Micro 18R refrigerated microcentrifuge. In addition, the well insulated rotor chamber provides very quiet operation.

The control panel of the Micro 18R is a combination of knobs and digital displays. A conversion program allows the display of real time rpm or rcf while the rotor is spinning. Acceleration and deceleration rates may be set to slow for more delicate samples, or fast, for quicker acceleration and more robust samples.

Timed or continuous operation may be selected on the keypad. A quick button allows for short runs at preset speeds - the unit will run only while the button is pressed. The reliable refrigeration
system keeps samples cool while processing.

The Micro 18R is supplied complete with a 24 place rotor. The rotor will accept 1.5 and 2.0 ml tubes directly. Adapters, sold separately, allow the tube cavities to hold smaller tubes.		 Specifications
