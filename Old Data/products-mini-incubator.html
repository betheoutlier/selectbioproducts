Mini Incubator
The Mini Incubator is a small unit designed for personal use, small laboratories and classrooms. The Mini Incubator features a broad temperature range to meet a variety of small sample applications.

Wrap-around heating elements ensure even heating and uniformity within the Mini Incubator. A hydraulic thermostat controls heating and temperature stability. A thermometer port in the top of the unit allows users to monitor temperature. The large window in the door gives a clear view of the contents which can include up to 48 petri dishes or 28 standard microplates. Shelves can be placed at two
points within the corrosion resistant chamber. A
cord port allows small mixers to be placed in
the incubator.		 Specifications
