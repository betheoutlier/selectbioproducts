Excel Electronic Multichannel Pipette
The multichannel Excel Electronic Pipettes retain all of the features of the single channel pipettes. They are available with an eight or twelve channel manifold for quick processing of samples in multiwell plates.

Program features of the multichannel pipettes include standard and reverse pipetting, multiple and sequential dispensing, mixing of samples and serial dilutions. Parameters are easily set with one hand using the keypad while operations are shown on the display. For easy reading, the display slants away from the user.

Excel Electronic Pipettes are fully motorized, guaranteeing excellent accuracy and reproducibility. The units are auto calibrating for an extra measure of confidence.

The slim body design of the Excel pipettes makes them easy to hold. The multichannel pipettes are well balanced and surprisingly lightweight. A small motion of the index finger is all that is required to activate the plunger, reducing the risk of Repetitive Stress Injuries (RSI).

A rechargeable Lithium ion battery powers the pipettes. They have a memory for up to nine sets of operating parameters and settings for aspirating and dispensing speed.		 Specifications
