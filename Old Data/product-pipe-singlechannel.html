SelectPette Autoclavable Pipettes
SelectPette Pipettes provide the highest levels of comfort and safety for the user, while providing exceptional accuracy and reproducibility.

The slim body of the SelectPette pipettes feels comfortable in your hand. The pipettes are well balanced and lightweight. All controls are easy to access. For convenience, the volume can be adjusted with the slightly recessed micrometer or by turning the plunger button. Chemical resistant internal and external components lend durability to these pipettes. For sterility, the entire pipette is autoclavable.

Quality pistons and Teflon seals provide for smooth, reproducible pipette operation. Each pipette is factory calibrated and individually tested for performance. SelectPette pipettes have been designed to maintain their calibration, even after repeated use and autoclaving. A key is included with each pipette for easy in house calibration and ISO/GLP compliance.

SelectPette pipettes are designed for universal tip fit. The shaft is tapered, while the height of the stainless steel ejector is adjustable. The top portion of the ejector is padded, for easy removal without pinching fingers. A selection of stands and clips are available for storing the pipettes when not in use.		 Specifications
