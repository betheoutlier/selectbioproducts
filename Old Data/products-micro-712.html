 Force 712
The Force 712 is a digital microcentrifuge designed for low to medium speed applications.

Operation of the unit is controlled by a microprocessor while time and rpm/rcf are set and displayed digitally. Airflow patterns within the chamber are engineered to keep samples cool and reduce rotor noise. The centrifuge is supplied complete with a removable, autoclavable rotor. The rotor accepts twelve 1.5 ml tubes directly and smaller tubes with adapters, sold separately. The small size and low profile of the Force 717 make it convenient for use on the bench, under a hood or in a refrigerated environment.

All of the Force microliter centrifuges are supplied complete with a removable, autoclavable rotor. The 512 and 712 models accept twelve 1.5 ml tubes. The 1418 accepts eighteen 1.5 ml tubes. Adapters are available for spinning smaller tubes.
