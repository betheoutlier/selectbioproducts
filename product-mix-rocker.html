Rock-it Platform Rocker
Whether your need is for simple rocking or the more complex action of a nutating mixer, SelectBioProducts has the instrument for you.

The Rock-it Platform Rocker features variable speed and an adjustable tilt angle. For staining fragile gels,a gentle wave motion can be chosen. More vigorous conditions can be set for washing sturdier mem-branes. Tilt angle and speed are changed simply by turning the associated knobs.

Both single and double platform models are available. Platforms are made of stainless steel with a rubber mat to keep sample containers in place. A heavy duty motor powers loads of up to 5 kilograms while permanently lubricated ball bearings provide smooth, maintenance free operation.
