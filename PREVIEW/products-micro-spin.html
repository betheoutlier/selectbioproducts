  MicroForce Spin Prep
Perfect for drying microarray slides, The Force SpinPrep is a personal sized unit that requires minimal bench space.

The rotor in the Force SpinPrep is fitted with two slide cassettes that catch liquid shed from the slides. Each cassette accommodates one standard sized slide. Slides can be dried in approximately 10 seconds. Quick acceleration and a smooth stop eliminate splashing that can cause artifacts to appear on the slides.

Similar in operation to the Force Mini, the SpinPrep can be started and stopped by the on/off power switch. Operation of the rotor may also be started simply by closing the lid of the centrifuge while the switch is in the on position. Pressing the lid release button stops operation.

The Force SpinPrep is suitable for use in the cold room.		 Specifications
