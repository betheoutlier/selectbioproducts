 Force Micro 512

The Force 512, 712 and 1418 are digital microliter centrifuges designed for low to medium speed applications.

Operation of these units is controlled by a microprocessor while time and rpm/rcf are set and displayed digitally. Air flow patterns within the chambers of the microliter centrifuges are designed to keep samples cool and reduce rotor noise. In addition, the larger model (1418) includes a brushless, maintenance free motor for faster acceleration and smooth operation.

All of the Force microliter centrifuges are supplied complete with a removable, autoclavable rotor. The 512 and 712 models accept twelve 1.5 ml tubes. The 1418 accepts eighteen 1.5 ml tubes. Adapters are available for spinning smaller tubes.

The small size of these units make them convenient for use on the bench, under a hood, or in refrigerated environments.		 Specifications
